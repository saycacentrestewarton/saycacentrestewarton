Gillian O'kane, Pilates

I trained as a Pilates instructor through the Australian Physiotherapy and Pilates Institute in 2016 and have since delivered a variety of classes throughout the West of Scotland.

Tagged

I qualified as a Chartered Physiotherapist in 2011 and have worked extensively within the NHS. I gained valuable experience as a junior physiotherapist within Women’s Health in 2014 where my love and journey into pelvic health began. Specialising in Pelvic Health in 2015 I continue my journey as a senior member of staff within the NHS and have since opened my own private practice in January 2019.

I have recently started classical Pilates training where I will continue to develop myself, and the clients I teach, via both one-to-one sessions and group classes.

You can find me in SAYCA community centre Stewarton on the following days/times:

  • Thursday 7 – 8pm
  • Thursday 8 – 9pm
  • Friday 10 – 11am
